Our Story

Built in the rain, on purpose.

Wetcoast Studio started in a Railtown workroom in 2019, cutting latex on a folding table between shifts at a costume shop. The city's wet-look weather felt like the obvious muse — six months of rain, glossed streets, and a fashion scene that wanted something bolder than another raincoat.

Every piece is still cut, glued, and seam-checked in that same neighbourhood, in small batches, by a team of four. We source latex sheet from small-batch mills, avoid deadstock waste by cutting to order, and test every seam by hand before it ships.

Care & FAQ

Good to know

How does latex sizing work?

Latex has zero stretch memory tolerance for guesswork, so we size to real body measurements rather than standard S/M/L. Every product page includes a measurement guide, and made-to-measure is available at checkout for a small upcharge.

How do I care for latex clothing?

Hand wash in lukewarm water with a mild, oil-free soap, air dry away from direct sunlight, then polish with a silicone-based latex shine spray. Store on a padded hanger or flat, away from metal and direct heat.

What's your shipping and returns policy?

Orders ship within 5–7 business days (made-to-measure: 2–3 weeks). Standard sizes are returnable within 14 days unworn and with tags attached; made-to-measure pieces are final sale.

Do you offer custom colours or commissions?

Yes — get in touch through the contact form with reference imagery and we'll follow up with a quote and timeline, usually within three business days.
